Unicorn

A long time ago, people believed that unicorn horns contain magical powers and when crushed into fine powder, it can heal all sicknesses including the plague. People would also believe that unicorns stab the hearts of dishonest people. Unicorns can easily kill elephants because of their super strength and strong horn. The most known way to capture a unicorn is have a pure and innocent lady to lay beside a tree and the unicorn will rest its head on her lap, then the hunter can capture it. To be a unicorn hunter you need a unicorn license Even if you capture a unicorn you will never be able to tame it unless you raise it as soon as it is born or befriend it with faith and purity. Unicorns can be found in a forest with brilliant green leaves and beautiful flowers near a pond.

Mermaid

Sailors once mistook manatees for a mermaid. Aquamarines are from mermaid tears. Legend has it, a fertility goddess (Atargatis) dove into a lake to become a fish but only half her body transformed. Some people believe that humans evolved from mermaids/mermen. There has been sightings of mermaids being kind and some that are mischievous.
